Frequently asked questions

How much does it cost to list my skills?

Listing is completely free. SkillConnect SV never charges commissions or per-lead fees — clients contact you directly.

How do I get the verified badge?

After submitting your profile, our team confirms your identity and a sample of your work. Verified providers appear higher in search results.

Can I update my rates or availability later?

Yes. You can change your hourly rate, availability and bio at any time from your profile.

What makes a great profile?

A specific headline, a clear list of skills, fair pricing and a friendly bio that explains your experience. Profiles with all of these receive the most inquiries.
