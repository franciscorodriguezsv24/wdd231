SkillConnect SV

Local Freelance Services Marketplace - Site Plan

1. Site Name

SkillConnect SV

Reason for Selection

The name "SkillConnect SV" was chosen because it clearly communicates the platform's core purpose - connecting people with skills to those who need services. "Skill" represents the talented freelancers and their abilities, while "Connect" emphasizes the platform's role as a bridge between service providers and clients. "SV" stands for El Salvador, anchoring the platform to its local community focus. The name is memorable, professional, and immediately tells visitors what the site offers.

Potential Domain: skillconnect-sv.com

2. Site Purpose

SkillConnect SV serves as a comprehensive platform that connects local freelancers and skilled individuals in El Salvador with community members who need their services. The site will:

  • Showcase Local Talent: Provide a directory where freelancers can display their skills, experience, portfolio samples, and availability
  • Enable Service Discovery: Allow users to browse, search, and filter service providers by category (home repair, tutoring, graphic design, pet care, photography, tech support, etc.)
  • Facilitate Connections: Provide contact information and inquiry forms so clients can reach out to service providers directly
  • Support Community Growth: Help skilled individuals find work opportunities while giving community members access to affordable, local services
  • Educate New Providers: Offer guidance on how to create effective profiles and present skills professionally

3. Scenarios

The following scenarios represent questions that target audience members might ask when visiting SkillConnect SV:

1

Client Perspective

"I need someone to fix a leaky faucet this weekend. How can I find a reliable plumber in my area who is available on short notice?"

Answer: The Service Providers Directory allows filtering by category (Home Repair), availability, and service area to quickly find qualified local plumbers.

2

Freelancer Perspective

"I'm a graphic designer looking for freelance work. How do I get listed on this platform and what information should I include in my profile?"

Answer: The "Post Your Skills" page provides step-by-step guidance on creating an effective profile, including tips for showcasing work and a submission form.

3

Parent Perspective

"My child is struggling with math. Can I find a local tutor who specializes in middle school mathematics and has good reviews?"

Answer: Users can filter the directory by "Tutoring" category and view detailed profiles showing specializations, experience levels, and client testimonials.

Accent Color

Amber

Usage: Featured badges, rating stars, important notifications, and hover states. Draws attention to key elements.

Text Dark

Charcoal Gray

Usage: Primary body text, headings, and important content. Ensures excellent readability.

Background Light

#F9FAFB

Snow White

Usage: Page backgrounds, card backgrounds, and content containers. Provides clean, open feel.

5. Typography

Heading Font: Poppins

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

Weights: 500 (Medium), 600 (Semi-Bold), 700 (Bold)

Usage: All headings (h1-h6), site name/logo, navigation links, button text, and card titles. Poppins is a geometric sans-serif that is modern, friendly, and highly legible at all sizes.

Body Font: Inter

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

Weights: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (Semi-Bold)

Usage: Body paragraphs, form labels, descriptions, metadata, and general content text. Inter was designed specifically for computer screens and provides excellent readability for longer text passages.

Typography Hierarchy Examples

H1: Main Page Title (Poppins Bold, 2.5rem)

H2: Section Heading (Poppins Semi-Bold, 1.75rem)

H3: Card/Subsection Title (Poppins Medium, 1.25rem)

Body Text: This is how regular paragraph text will appear on the site. Inter provides clean, professional readability. (Inter Regular, 1rem)

Small Text/Metadata: Supporting information like dates and categories (Inter Regular, 0.875rem)

7. Page Overview

Page 1: Home
